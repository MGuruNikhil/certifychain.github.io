Frequently Asked Questions

What is CertifyChain?

CertifyChain is a decentralized certification platform built on blockchain technology. It enables organizations to issue tamper-proof digital certificates to individuals, ensuring the authenticity and integrity of the certification process.

How does CertifyChain ensure the security of certificates?

CertifyChain leverages blockchain technology and IPFS to create tamper-proof and immutable digital certificates. The certificates are stored on a decentralized network, making them resistant to censorship and single-point failures.

Who can issue certificates on CertifyChain?

Any organization or individual can issue certificates on CertifyChain. The platform is open and decentralized, allowing anyone to participate in the certification process without relying on intermediaries.

How can I verify a certificate issued on CertifyChain?

You can verify a certificate issued on CertifyChain by accessing the platform and entering the certificate's unique identifier. The platform will retrieve the certificate from the decentralized network and display its details, allowing you to confirm its authenticity.

Is CertifyChain open-source?

Yes, CertifyChain is an open-source project. The source code is available on our GitHub repository, and we welcome contributions from the community to improve and enhance the platform.
